## Scope — Reciprocal Relocation, 130 Desktop Technology Setups

### Building 1 (5th Floor) → Building 4 (3rd Floor)
- Disconnect and break down 65 three-monitor technology setups.
- Pack and transport to Building 4, 3rd Floor.
- Install at 65 desks.
- Complete wire and cable management.
- Confirm relocated equipment is properly connected and operational.
- Each setup: 3 monitors, monitor-arm assembly (excluding the existing vertical post), docking station, associated power/data cabling.

### Building 4 (3rd Floor) → Building 1 (5th Floor)
- Disconnect and break down 65 two-monitor technology setups.
- Pack and transport to Building 1, 5th Floor.
- Install at 65 desks.
- Complete wire and cable management.
- Confirm relocated equipment is properly connected and operational.
- Each setup: 2 monitors, monitor-arm assembly (excluding the existing vertical post), docking station, associated power/data cabling.

## Monitor-Arm Installation Detail

Existing vertical monitor poles remain installed at the desks. Monitor arms convert between
3-monitor and 2-monitor configurations by loosening clamps and repositioning/exchanging arm
assemblies — only the monitor arms, monitors, docking stations, cables, and related desktop
tech need to physically relocate. Goosehead's photographs identify the clamp/connection
points to loosen during conversion.
