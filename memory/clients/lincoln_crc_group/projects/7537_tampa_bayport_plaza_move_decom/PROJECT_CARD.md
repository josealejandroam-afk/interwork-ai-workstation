## Move Scope

Employees pack personal belongings into supplied crates/banker boxes (~2-3 per employee), label them, leave ready for movers. Employees also pack any kitchen contents they want retained.

Identified items to move:
- Employee crates and packed contents
- Mini refrigerator from the conference room
- Contents of the large and small storage rooms
- ~6-8 small plants
- AED box
- Fluorescent entry signage
- Miscellaneous awards from the entryway
- ~3 ancillary coffee/end tables
- Other misc FF&E identified during final walkthrough

Mainly a contents move, not a major furniture relocation.

## Decommission Scope

Begins immediately after the move. Per lease interpretation discussed in the project meeting, CRC-installed items (including cabling) must be removed from the existing space.

Expected elements:
- Removal of remaining furniture and misc contents
- Removal of trash and unwanted items
- Removal of low-voltage cabling, routed/removed back to the building demarcation point in the basement
- Removal of CRC-installed fixtures/equipment as required by the lease
- Loading/removal of decommissioned materials through the loading dock
- Final walkthrough and acceptance after completion

Detailed decommission inventory not yet finalized.
