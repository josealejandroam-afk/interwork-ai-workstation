# Equipment Specs

General-purpose equipment dimensions/specs for use across projects. Not project-specific —
see individual project cards for how many units were used on a given job.

## C-Bin

- **Overall (exterior):** ~48 in. L × 24 in. W × 28 in. H
- **Usable interior space:** ~46 in. L × 23 in. W × 28 in. H
- Typically sits on a wood dolly or pallet-jack runner underneath.

## Standard Move Equipment & Materials (typical office move)

**Pre-delivered equipment** — often dropped at site before the move begins so the client and crew can prepare:
- C-bins
- Dollies
- Panel carts
- Reusable moving crates, when needed

**Packing and protection materials:**
- Bubble wrap
- Masonite floor protection
- Corrugated cardboard
- Shrink wrap

**Scoping note:** treat crates as job-dependent (quantity/need varies per project — confirm per
job, as seen with the increased-to-10-crates case on project 7549). C-bins, dollies, panel
carts, and basic protection materials are part of the usual move-planning checklist for any
typical office move.
